About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Yacht Design from the West Pomeranian University of Technology in 2024. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Electronics and Telecommunications at the Silesian University of Technology.
I am a member of several student research groups and organizations, including Silesian Aerospace Technologies and Trådstudio.

I am also a certified sailing yacht skipper (up to 18 m LOA), a sailing instructor, and a certified camp counselor.

My competencies and experience span a wide range of fields, including electronics, radio communication, sailing, FDM and SLA 3D printing, retro photography, and skiing.

I speak English at a communicative level, as well as some German and Russian.

Featured Projects

TrådJacht

In Development

The main costs in maritime transport are fuel prices and crew labor costs. In addition, although it is the most efficient form of transportation, maritime transport is responsible for 3% of global CO2 emissions and a significant share of global methane emissions. Reducing operating costs and greenhouse gas emissions is possible, through the use of sails, which could be connected into an automated steering system. Our project involves developing a model of an autonomous sailing vessel, capable of independent travel from point A to point B, without the use of any kind of a motorised propulsion system.
