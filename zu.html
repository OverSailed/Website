About Me

Hi, my name is Zuzanna Sczyrba.

I have two passions in life: sailing and film photography. In 2025, I discovered that they can be combined when I made a documentary film from a month-long voyage of the Polish sailing vessel STS Generał Zaruski to the Arctic.

Now I am trying to combine them again.

Since 2023, I have been studying cinematography at film school. I began my sailing journey in 2016.

How I See Things

Things are easiest for me to explain through drawings. I am probably a visual thinker, but I also like listening to audiobooks and to people. I believe that an image can communicate everything — maybe that is why I am a cinematographer.
